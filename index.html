About Us

Apa Itu Symphonie Class?

Symphonie Class is a class that focuses on contempory music such a pop, rock, jazz, and other modern genres. Some of the programs we offer at Symphonie Class are Theory Class, Annual Home Concert and many more. We provide the international Standar Curiculum fro Premiere to Grade 8.

Symphonie not only provides music education, but also educates people about the importance of mental health.

Theory Class

Music theory, like language, enables us to understand the structure and meaning behind a musical composition. Secondly, music theory allows us to speak with other musicians in a common language. It serves as a short-hand way of refering to important point in the music.
